About JamboShop

Kenya's fastest growing e-commerce platform, connecting buyers and sellers across the nation

Our Story

Founded in 2018 in Nairobi, JamboShop began as a small online marketplace for local artisans to sell their products. What started as a passion project quickly grew into Kenya's premier e-commerce platform.

Today, we connect thousands of Kenyan businesses with millions of customers across all 47 counties, offering everything from electronics to fresh groceries delivered right to your doorstep.

Our Journey

2018

Founded in Nairobi

Started as a small platform with just 20 local sellers focusing on handmade crafts and fashion.

2019

Expanded to Electronics

Added electronics category and introduced next-day delivery in Nairobi.

2020

Nationwide Reach

Expanded delivery network to cover all 47 counties in Kenya.

2022

Mobile App Launch

Launched our mobile app with M-Pesa integration, reaching 100,000 downloads in 3 months.

2023

Market Leader

Recognized as Kenya's fastest growing e-commerce platform with over 5,000 active sellers.
